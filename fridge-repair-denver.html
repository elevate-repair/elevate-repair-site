Refrigerator Repair Services We Offer

Our technicians handle every type of residential refrigerator repair, from standard top-freezer models to French door, side-by-side, and built-in units. We carry common replacement parts on our service vehicles so most repairs can be completed during the first visit, minimizing the time your food spends at risk.

Compressor and sealed-system repairs are among the most involved refrigerator fixes we perform. When a compressor fails or a refrigerant leak develops, your unit cannot maintain proper temperatures. We test sealed-system components, replace faulty compressors, and recharge refrigerant to factory specifications. We also replace start relays, overload protectors, and run capacitors that support compressor operation.

Defrost system failures are another frequent issue. Modern frost-free refrigerators rely on a defrost heater, defrost thermostat, and defrost timer or control board to prevent ice buildup on the evaporator coils. When any of these parts fail, frost accumulates and blocks airflow, causing the refrigerator compartment to warm up. We diagnose which component has failed and replace it to restore proper cooling cycles.

We also repair and replace evaporator fan motors, condenser fan motors, thermistors, temperature control boards, door gaskets, ice makers, water inlet valves, and water filters. Whether your refrigerator is a Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, GE, Frigidaire, Maytag, KitchenAid, Bosch, Sub-Zero, or any other brand, our team has the training and parts access to get it running correctly. Common issues we fix include refrigerator not cooling, ice maker not working, and refrigerator leaking water.
