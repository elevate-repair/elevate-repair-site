Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our appliance repair services.

Do you offer same-day service?
Yes! If you call before noon, we can usually have a technician at your door the same day. We are available 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays, so you never have to wait long to get your appliance fixed.
What is your service area?
We serve the entire Denver metro area, including Aurora, Lakewood, Evergreen, Golden, Boulder, Westminster, Arvada, Littleton, Englewood, Centennial, and more. Visit our service areas page for the full list of cities we cover.
How much will my repair cost?
Every repair starts with an $89 diagnostic fee, which covers the technician's visit and a complete assessment of the problem. If you choose to proceed with the repair, that $89 is applied as a credit toward your total cost. We always provide an upfront quote before any work begins, so there are no surprises. Check our coupons page for current discounts.
Is your work guaranteed?
Absolutely. Every completed repair comes with a 60-day warranty covering both parts and labor. If the same issue comes back within 60 days, we return and fix it at no additional cost to you.
What appliances do you repair?
We repair most major household appliances, including refrigerators, freezers, ice makers, dishwashers, ovens, stoves, ranges, washers, dryers, and microwaves. If it is in your kitchen or laundry room, there is a good chance we can fix it.
What brands do you service?
We service all major appliance brands, from everyday names like Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, and LG to premium brands like Sub-Zero, Viking, and Thermador. See our full brands list for more details.
How do I schedule a repair?
You have two easy options. Call us directly at (720) 575-8432 and we will set up an appointment over the phone. Or fill out our online booking form to schedule at your convenience and save $25 on your repair.
Do you charge a trip fee?
Our service call fee is $89. This covers the technician's trip to your home and a full diagnosis of the problem. If you approve the repair and we complete it, the $89 is applied as a credit toward your total bill. You only pay the service call fee separately if you decide not to proceed with the repair.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit cards, and debit cards. Payment is collected after the repair is completed to your satisfaction.
What if I need to cancel my appointment?
We understand that plans change. Just give us a call at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment and we will happily reschedule at no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a $50 fee. You can read the full details on our cancellation policy page.

Have another question? Call (720) 575-8432 or contact us — we are happy to help!
