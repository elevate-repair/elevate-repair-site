Why Your Refrigerator Is Leaking Water in Highlands Ranch

Refrigerator leaks can be a common nuisance in Highlands Ranch, largely due to a variety of factors that might be at play. One of the most prevalent causes is a clogged or freezing defrost drain. When debris accumulates or ice forms, it can block the drain, causing water to collect and eventually leak out.

Another frequent issue is the water filter not being installed correctly. In some Highlands Ranch homes, hard water can contribute to deposits that hinder a secure filter fit, leading to leaks. Additionally, the gasket or door seal might wear out over time, especially with frequent use, causing condensation to form and drip down.

The unique climate in Highlands Ranch, with its dry air, can also affect the refrigerator's efficiency, sometimes causing more rapid wear on components like seals and hoses. Understanding these conditions helps in preventing leaks and maintaining your appliance in top condition.

What Our Technicians Inspect

  • Defrost Drain: Checked for blockages or ice build-up, which can cause water leakage.
  • Water Filter: Ensured to be properly installed and free from hard water deposits.
  • Door Gasket: Inspected for any wear and tear that might cause condensation leaks.
  • Water Line: Examined for cracks or loose connections that could lead to dripping.
  • Ice Maker Assembly: Checked for leaks originating from connections or the ice maker itself.
  • Drip Pan: Evaluated to ensure it's positioned correctly and not overflowing.
