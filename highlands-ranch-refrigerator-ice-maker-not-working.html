Common Questions About Refrigerator Ice Maker Not Working Issues

Why is my ice maker producing small or misshapen ice cubes? This can occur due to low water pressure or a faulty water inlet valve. Ensuring these components are working correctly can resolve the issue.

What should I do if my ice maker leaks water inside the freezer? A common cause is a misaligned water line or a clogged drain. Checking these areas can help prevent further leakage.

How often should the water filter be changed? To maintain optimal ice maker performance, replace the water filter every six months or as recommended by the manufacturer.
