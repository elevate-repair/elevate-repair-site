Common Electrolux Repair Issues

Electrolux front-load washers are the most common Electrolux appliance we service in Denver. The door boot seal, which is the rubber gasket between the door and the drum, is the primary failure point. Over time, this seal can develop tears, mold buildup, or warping that causes water to leak onto the floor during the wash or spin cycle. Small objects like coins, hair pins, or underwire from bras can puncture the boot seal, creating an immediate leak. We replace the door boot seal and inspect the drain system to ensure no debris has migrated into the pump or drain hose.

Electrolux dryers develop drum bearing wear as a common failure pattern, particularly in units that have been in service for several years. When the rear drum bearing wears out, the dryer produces a loud squealing, grinding, or rumbling noise during operation. The drum may also wobble or scrape against the housing. Replacing the drum bearing requires disassembling the dryer to access the rear of the drum, and we typically replace the drum support rollers and belt at the same time to prevent a return visit for related wear items.

Electrolux ovens and ranges can experience oven igniter failures on gas models. The igniter is a silicon carbide element that glows red-hot to open the gas safety valve and ignite the burner. As the igniter ages, it draws less current and may glow but not reach the amperage threshold required to open the gas valve. The result is a gas oven that will not heat up despite the igniter visibly glowing. This is one of the most commonly misdiagnosed oven issues, and we carry replacement igniters for Electrolux gas ovens and ranges.

Electrolux refrigerators can develop evaporator fan motor failures, which result in the freezer working normally while the refrigerator compartment warms up. The evaporator fan circulates cold air from the freezer into the refrigerator section, and when it stops spinning, only the freezer stays cold. Electrolux dishwashers may experience wash pump motor issues or control board failures. We service all Electrolux kitchen and laundry appliances and carry common replacement parts for the most frequently needed repairs.
