Why Your Dryer Is Making Loud Noise in Westminster

Experiencing a loud dryer can disrupt the peace in your Westminster home. One common cause is a worn drum bearing, which may produce a grinding noise. Over time, this component can wear out due to the constant rotation of the drum, especially in homes that frequently use their dryers.

Another frequent culprit is the drive belt. In the dry climate of Westminster, belts can become brittle and crack. When this happens, they may produce a thumping noise as they slip off their intended path. Regular inspection can help prevent this issue before it escalates.

Additionally, blower wheels can become clogged with lint or debris, leading to loud rattling sounds. Westminster homeowners should ensure their dryers are regularly cleaned to avoid such issues. The unique climate here can also contribute to the accumulation of static, exacerbating such problems.

What Our Technicians Inspect

  • Drum Bearings — We check for wear and tear, ensuring smooth drum rotation.
  • Drive Belt — Inspected for cracks and alignment to prevent slipping and noise.
  • Blower Wheel — We clear any debris to maintain proper airflow and reduce rattling.
  • Idler Pulley — Examined for wear to avoid squealing noises during operation.
  • Motor — Assessed for functioning properly to ensure quiet operation.
  • Drum Seal — Checked for gaps or wear that might cause thumping or scraping noises.
