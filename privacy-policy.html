Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you contact Elevate Repair or submit a service request, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and phone number (provided through our contact forms)
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Service address for appliance repair appointments
  • Appliance details related to your repair request

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Schedule and perform appliance repair services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only with our technicians as needed to complete your service appointment.
