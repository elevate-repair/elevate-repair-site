Why Your Refrigerator Is Not Cooling in Westminster

Refrigerators in Westminster homes can face various issues that cause them to stop cooling. One common cause is a malfunctioning thermostat, which can prevent the appliance from maintaining the correct temperature. Another frequent issue is blocked vents, which can result in restricted airflow and uneven cooling. These problems can be exacerbated by Westminster's fluctuating climate, leading to increased strain on your refrigerator.

In addition, dirty condenser coils can significantly impact the cooling efficiency of your refrigerator. These coils, located at the back or beneath your appliance, can accumulate dust and debris, hindering their ability to release heat. With Westminster's seasonal variations in temperature, keeping these coils clean becomes crucial to ensure optimal performance.

Lastly, a faulty compressor or evaporator fan can contribute to cooling issues. Both components are essential for circulating coolant and maintaining the desired temperature inside your refrigerator. If either part fails, it can disrupt the cooling process, leaving your appliance struggling to keep your food fresh.

What Our Technicians Inspect

  • Thermostat: Technicians check if the thermostat is calibrated correctly and operational to ensure accurate temperature control.
  • Condenser Coils: We inspect and clean the condenser coils to improve heat dissipation and efficiency.
  • Vents: Ensuring that vents are free from obstructions to promote proper airflow within the refrigerator.
  • Compressor: We evaluate the compressor's condition and functionality, as it plays a vital role in the cooling process.
  • Evaporator Fan: Technicians examine the evaporator fan to confirm it's running smoothly and circulating air effectively.
  • Seals and Gaskets: We inspect door seals and gaskets to prevent cold air leaks that can compromise cooling efficiency.
