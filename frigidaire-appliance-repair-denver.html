Common Frigidaire Repair Issues

Frigidaire refrigerator ice maker failures are the most frequent service call we handle for this brand in Denver. The ice maker module itself can fail, but more often the issue is a frozen fill tube that prevents water from reaching the ice mold. This is caused by a defective inlet valve that drips after the fill cycle, allowing water to freeze inside the tube. In other cases, the ice maker simply stops producing ice due to a failed optical sensor or harvest motor. We also see compressor relay clicks on older Frigidaire side-by-side models, where the relay attempts to start the compressor and fails repeatedly, producing a clicking sound every few minutes while the refrigerator warms up.

Frigidaire Gallery series models use electronic control boards that manage both the cooking and cooling functions. These control boards can develop faults due to power surges or component fatigue, resulting in error codes on the display, unresponsive controls, or appliances that fail to start. We carry replacement control boards for the most common Gallery refrigerator and range models. On Frigidaire dishwashers, the most common issue is a failed drain pump or a clogged drain hose, which leaves standing water in the bottom of the tub after the wash cycle completes.

Frigidaire washers and dryers are generally reliable, but we see a consistent pattern of lid switch failures on their top-load washers. When the lid switch fails, the washer will not advance to the spin cycle. Frigidaire dryers commonly develop issues with the thermal fuse, which blows when the exhaust vent is restricted or the lint trap is not cleaned regularly. A blown thermal fuse stops the dryer from producing heat, or in some models, prevents the dryer from starting entirely. We test the thermal fuse, inspect the venting system, and replace the fuse to restore operation.

Frigidaire ovens and ranges can experience igniter wear on gas models, where the igniter glows but does not get hot enough to open the gas valve. Electric models may have failed bake or broil elements. In the Gallery and Professional lines, the convection fan motor can develop bearing noise or stop spinning altogether, which eliminates even heat distribution. We service all Frigidaire cooking products and carry common igniters, elements, and control boards.
