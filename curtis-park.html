Dependable Appliance Repair for Curtis Park Homeowners

Curtis Park stretches between Downing Street and the railroad tracks, roughly from 24th Street up to 33rd Avenue. The neighborhood earned a spot on the National Register of Historic Places thanks to its remarkable collection of Queen Anne and Denver Square homes dating to the 1880s and 1890s. Many of these residences have been lovingly restored, and their kitchens often blend period charm with modern appliances. Elevate Repair understands how to work in these unique spaces without disturbing original woodwork or plaster.

Newer construction has also found a home in Curtis Park, with contemporary townhomes and duplexes filling in along Stout Street and California Street. These units typically feature modern built-in appliances, compact laundry setups, and energy-efficient dishwashers. Our technicians stay current on the latest models from every major manufacturer, so whether your appliance is brand new or a decade old, we have the expertise to diagnose and repair it on the spot.

Transparency matters in Curtis Park, where neighbors share recommendations and word travels fast. Elevate Repair charges an $89 diagnostic fee that is credited toward your repair, and we provide a firm quote before any wrench is turned. We schedule appointments seven days a week, 7am to 10pm, so you can find a slot that fits between trips to the Curtis Park Farmers Market or a walk through Mestizo-Curtis Park.

Our shop is located at 1500 N Grant St, just a short drive down Downing from Curtis Park. We can usually be at your door within the hour. Every repair we complete comes with our 60-day warranty, so you can trust the work will hold up long after we leave.
