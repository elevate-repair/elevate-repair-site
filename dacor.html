Common Dacor Appliance Issues

Dacor wall ovens frequently experience issues with their touchscreen LCD control panels, particularly on older Modernist models. The SteamAssist system can develop mineral buildup in the steam generator, especially in areas with hard water like parts of the Denver metro area. We also commonly repair gas igniter failures, convection fan motor burnout, and temperature sensor drift on Dacor ranges.

Dacor cooktops sometimes develop issues with their sealed burner caps, simmer burner functionality, and electronic ignition modules. Their column refrigerators may experience compressor relay failures, defrost timer problems, and door seal degradation. We diagnose every issue thoroughly before recommending repairs and always provide transparent pricing.
