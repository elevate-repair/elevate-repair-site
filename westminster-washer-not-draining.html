Why Your Washer Is Not Draining in Westminster

Living in Westminster, homeowners might encounter washers that refuse to drain properly. This issue often stems from clogged drain hoses or malfunctioning pumps. The local climate, with its dry air and occasional dust, can exacerbate clogs over time, leading to water drainage problems in your washing machine.

Another common cause is a faulty lid switch, which can prevent the washer from entering the spin cycle. This issue is prevalent in many Westminster homes, especially in older machines that might not have been serviced regularly. Additionally, small objects left in pockets can get trapped in the pump, causing it to malfunction.

In Westminster, where energy-efficient appliances are popular, an improperly leveled washer can also cause drainage issues. If the washer isn't level, the water might not flow as intended, leading to standing water. Regular maintenance by professional technicians like those at Elevate Repair can help identify and fix these issues promptly.

What Our Technicians Inspect

  • Drain Hose: Checks for clogs or kinks that prevent proper water flow.
  • Pump: Ensures the pump is functioning and free of obstructions.
  • Lint Filter: Inspects and cleans the filter to facilitate smooth drainage.
  • Lid Switch: Verifies that the switch is operational and allows the spin cycle to engage.
  • Belt: Examines the belt for wear and ensures it's correctly tensioned.
  • Leveling: Confirms the washer is properly leveled to aid in efficient drainage.
