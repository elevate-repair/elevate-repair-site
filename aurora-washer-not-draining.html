Why Your Washer Is Not Draining in Aurora

A washer not draining can be a frustrating issue for any homeowner in Aurora. One common reason could be a clogged drain hose, often caused by debris or small clothing items obstructing the flow of water. Additionally, the pump could be malfunctioning due to wear and tear, particularly in older machines.

The geographical characteristics of Aurora can also play a role in these issues. The hard water prevalent in the area can lead to mineral buildup inside the washer components, potentially affecting the draining process. Proper maintenance and regular inspections can help mitigate these effects and keep your appliance running smoothly.

Another potential cause could be a faulty lid switch. This safety mechanism ensures that the washer does not operate with an open lid. If the switch is defective, it might prevent the washer from advancing to the draining cycle. Checking these components regularly can help avoid unexpected breakdowns.
