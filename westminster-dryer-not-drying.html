What Our Technicians Inspect

  • Lint Trap: A clogged lint trap can restrict airflow and reduce drying efficiency.
  • Vent System: Blockages in the vent system can cause moisture build-up and prolonged drying times.
  • Heating Element: A malfunctioning heating element can prevent the dryer from reaching necessary temperatures.
  • Thermostat: A faulty thermostat may cause the dryer to underheat or overheat.
  • Drum Seals: Damaged seals can lead to heat escaping, affecting the drying process.
  • Moisture Sensor: If the sensor is faulty, it might not detect when clothes are dry, leading to extended cycles.
