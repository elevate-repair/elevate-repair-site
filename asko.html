Asko Services We Offer

Asko Refrigerator Repair

Why Choose Elevate Repair for Your Asko Appliances

Asko has been manufacturing appliances in Sweden since 1950, born from a farmer's desire to ease his mother's laundry burden. That focus on practical, durable design continues today, with Asko appliances known for their quiet operation, water efficiency, and clean Scandinavian aesthetics. Now part of the Gorenje Group, Asko maintains its commitment to sustainability and functional design that appeals to environmentally conscious Denver homeowners.

Our technicians are experienced with Asko's European-specification components, compact dimensions, and advanced features like heat pump drying and Turbo Combi dishwasher drying. Asko appliances use different engineering approaches than American brands, including unique drum suspension systems in washers, condensation-based dryer technology, and specialized dishwasher spray arm configurations. We understand these differences and repair them accordingly.

Asko owners value quality, efficiency, and longevity. We share those values in our repair approach: accurate diagnosis, genuine replacement parts, and thorough testing. Our 60-day warranty on all repairs gives you the peace of mind that your Asko appliance is fixed right.

Common Asko Appliance Issues

Asko dishwashers sometimes develop drainage issues caused by food particle buildup in the filter system and drain pump. The Turbo Combi Drying system, while highly effective, can experience fan motor or heating element failures over time. We also commonly repair door latch mechanisms, detergent dispenser assemblies, and control board issues that cause cycle interruptions or error codes on Asko dishwasher models.

Asko washers may experience door boot seal tears that cause leaks, drum bearing wear that creates noise during spin cycles, and drain pump failures. Their heat pump dryers can develop condenser lint buildup that reduces efficiency and refrigerant system issues that affect drying performance. For all Asko appliances, we use manufacturer-specified parts and follow Scandinavian engineering standards to ensure proper repair quality.
