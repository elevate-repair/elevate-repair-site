Why Your Dryer Is Not Spinning in Lakewood

Dryers that won't spin can be a common issue for homeowners in Lakewood. This problem often arises due to a variety of causes. A broken drive belt, which helps the drum to rotate, is a frequent culprit. When the belt wears out or breaks, the drum will not spin, leaving your clothes damp. Additionally, the motor can become faulty over time, especially when subjected to the varying climate conditions experienced in Lakewood.

Another cause could be a malfunctioning door switch. When the door switch is faulty, it may not signal the dryer to start spinning. Furthermore, issues like a jammed drum roller or a worn-out idler pulley can also lead to a non-spinning dryer. With the diverse range of homes in Lakewood, from new builds to mid-century designs, these issues can affect dryers of all ages and models.

Lastly, electrical problems, such as a blown thermal fuse or a tripped circuit breaker, can prevent the drum from spinning. Given Lakewood's varied weather, power surges might occasionally occur, impacting appliance performance.
