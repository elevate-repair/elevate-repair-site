Why Your Refrigerator Is Leaking Water in Westminster

Refrigerator leaks can be a common nuisance in Westminster homes, often due to a variety of underlying issues. One prevalent cause is a blocked defrost drain. Over time, food particles or debris can clog the drain hose, leading to water leakage. Given Westminster's variable climate, with its warm summers and cold winters, fluctuating temperatures can exacerbate these issues by causing ice build-up that blocks the drain.

Another frequent culprit is a damaged water inlet valve. This component controls the flow of water into the refrigerator, and if malfunctioning, it could result in leaks. Additionally, the water filter might be incorrectly installed or overdue for a change, contributing to water pooling at the base of your refrigerator. Westminster's hard water can also cause mineral deposits to accumulate, further complicating water flow and causing leaks.

Lastly, check your refrigerator's door seals. If they are worn or not sealing properly, warm air can enter, leading to excess condensation and leaks. The unique climate in Westminster could lead to seals expanding and contracting, thus wearing them out over time. Regular inspection and maintenance can help mitigate these issues before they lead to significant water damage in your kitchen.

What Our Technicians Inspect

  • Defrost Drain: Ensuring it’s clear of obstructions to prevent water back-up.
  • Water Inlet Valve: Checking for leaks or damage that could lead to water escaping.
  • Water Filter: Confirming it’s installed correctly and not overdue for replacement.
  • Drain Pan: Inspecting for cracks and ensuring it’s positioned correctly to collect condensation.
  • Door Seals: Examining for wear and proper sealing to prevent warm air intrusion.
  • Ice Maker: Checking connections and components for leaks or blockages.
