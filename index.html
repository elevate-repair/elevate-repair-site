Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day service?
Yes, in most cases we can provide same-day service for urgent repairs. Call us early in the day for the best availability.
What brands do you service?
We repair all major appliance brands including Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Maytag, Frigidaire, KitchenAid, Bosch, and many more.
How much does a repair cost?
Costs vary by repair type. We provide a diagnostic fee for the service call, then give you an upfront quote before starting any work. No hidden fees.

Yes, all repairs come with a 60-day warranty on parts and labor.

Our technicians will give you an honest assessment. If the repair cost is too high relative to the appliance value, we'll let you know.

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and debit cards for your convenience.
