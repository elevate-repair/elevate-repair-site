Our Repair Warranty

Quality repairs backed by a 60-day guarantee.

At Elevate Repair, we stand behind our work. Every completed appliance repair comes with a 60-day warranty covering both parts and labor. We believe in fixing things right the first time, and our warranty reflects that commitment.

What Our Warranty Covers

If the same issue we repaired reoccurs within 60 days of the original service date, we will return to your home and fix it at no additional charge. No hoops to jump through, no fine print to worry about. Just give us a call and we will take care of it.

We use quality replacement parts on every repair and our technicians are trained to diagnose problems thoroughly so you do not end up dealing with the same issue again. Our goal is always a one-visit fix.

Warranty Highlights

  • 60 Days of Coverage — Full protection starting from the date of your completed repair.
  • Parts and Labor Included — Both the replacement parts and the work to install them are covered.
  • Hassle-Free — Just Call Us — If the problem comes back, call (720) 575-8432 and we will schedule a return visit promptly.
  • No Paperwork Needed — We keep records of every repair, so you do not need to hold onto receipts or fill out forms.

Exclusions

Our warranty covers the specific repair that was performed. It does not extend to unrelated issues, problems caused by misuse or accidental damage, or failure due to external causes such as power surges, flooding, or pest damage. If a different component fails or a new issue arises, we are happy to come back and diagnose it — our standard service call rates and discounts would apply.

Our Promise to You

When you hire Elevate Repair, you can be confident that the job will be done right, with quality parts and experienced technicians. Our 60-day warranty is not just a policy — it is our way of showing that we truly stand behind our work.
